# Privacy policy — Remove Multi Titles (YouTube)

**Last updated:** 2026-03-30

## What this extension does

It remembers the video title you see the first time (on the player or in a list tile) and keeps showing that same title if YouTube tries to show a different one later (for example during A/B tests). Everything needed for that happens on your device.

## Data we collect

**None.** There are no accounts, no analytics, and no requests to our servers. We do not receive your browsing data.

## Where information is stored

Pinned titles are stored only in your browser’s **local extension storage** (`storage` API). It stays on your computer and is not synced or uploaded by this extension.

## Permissions

- **YouTube (`*://*.youtube.com/*`):** The extension only runs on YouTube sites so it can read and adjust title text on the watch/Shorts player and on list-style layouts (home, search, related, etc.). It does not run on other websites.
- **Storage:** Used only to save and read those pinned titles locally.

## Contact

For privacy questions, open an issue on the project repository (see the extension listing or `homepage_url` in the manifest).

## Changes

If this policy changes in a future version, the updated text will be published at the same public URL you use for store listings, with an updated date at the top.
